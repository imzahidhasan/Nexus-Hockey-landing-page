Hockey Training

The frequency of your hockey training sessions can greatly impact your skill development. While individual needs may vary, we generally recommend practicing at least 3 to 4 times a week for optimal improvement. Consistent and focused training sessions, including a mix of on-ice drills, off-ice workouts, and game simulations, contribute to enhancing your overall performance on the hockey rink.

Private Lessons

Our private lessons are tailored to meet your individual needs and goals. During the booking process, you can provide specific details about what you'd like to focus on during the lesson. Whether it's mastering a particular skill, getting extra help with challenging topics, or refining your technique, our instructors are here to customize the lesson to your preferences.

Ski Touring

Guided ski tours are available for individuals of all skill levels. While experienced skiers may choose to venture out on their own, beginners and those unfamiliar with the terrain may benefit from guided tours. Guides provide valuable insights into safety, technique, and the local environment, enhancing the overall experience.
